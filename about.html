The Founder

Fanuel Debebe

Apparel Engineer · Fashion Technologist · Cloud Engineer · Systems Architect

Fanuel Debebe's career is defined by an uncommon willingness to operate at the borders of disciplines — the boundary where the craft of apparel engineering meets the logic of distributed systems, and where fashion's cultural intelligence meets the rigour of cloud infrastructure.

Beginning with a foundation in apparel engineering, Fanuel developed a precision-first mindset — learning that every seam, every specification, every tolerance has consequence. That discipline translated directly into his approach to software systems and cloud architecture: every component must be purposeful, every integration must be resilient.

As a fashion technologist, he pioneered digital approaches to design pipelines — 3D prototyping, digital pattern engineering, and data-informed collection development. This work taught him that technology should serve creativity, not constrain it, and that the most powerful systems are invisible to the people who use them.

Korenty is the synthesis of these experiences — a consultancy that doesn't choose between strategic depth and technical capability, but demands both, simultaneously, in every engagement.

Journey

Building Toward
Something Different

A deliberate path through disciplines — each chapter adding a layer of capability that compounds into Korenty's hybrid authority.

2019
Apparel Engineering Foundation
Established a precision-first methodology through apparel engineering — developing pattern construction, structural garment analysis, and technical specification mastery that would later inform systems thinking.
2020
Fashion Technology Innovation
Pioneered digital fashion workflows — 3D prototyping, virtual sampling, and data-driven design pipelines — bridging physical craftsmanship with digital production infrastructure.
2021
Cloud Infrastructure Mastery
Deep-dived into cloud engineering: multi-region architectures, containerisation, serverless systems, and infrastructure-as-code. Applied the same precision of apparel construction to distributed systems design.
2022
Systems Architecture Expertise
Developed end-to-end systems architecture capability — designing scalable, resilient platforms that integrate creative workflows with enterprise-grade technical infrastructure.
2023
Korenty Founded
Launched Korenty as a hybrid strategic and digital infrastructure consultancy — the first firm built to operate simultaneously across brand strategy, fashion technology, and cloud systems architecture.
2024
Strategic Expansion
Expanded Korenty's client portfolio across international markets — delivering integrated engagements that combine identity design, digital transformation, and cloud infrastructure at enterprise scale.

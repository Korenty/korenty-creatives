The Founder

Fanuel Debebe

Apparel Engineer · Fashion Technologist · Cloud Engineer · Systems Architect · Entrepreneur

Fanuel Debebe's background spans disciplines that few people combine — apparel engineering and fashion technology that sharpened his design thinking and precision, followed by deep work in cloud engineering and systems architecture that built his technical authority.

His entrepreneurial journey — including founding GurshaHub, a tech startup — added a further dimension: the ability to think and operate as a builder. That founder mindset directly informs how Korenty approaches every engagement: analytical, growth-driven, and built for real-world outcomes.

Korenty was founded to bring this multi-disciplinary expertise to a specific class of clients — startups, influencers, public figures, and extraordinary personalities who need branding and digital systems of the highest order. The founder's breadth of expertise is not the service; it is what makes the service exceptional.

Every solution Korenty delivers is engineered with precision, designed for authority, and structured to scale — a direct expression of the disciplines that Fanuel has mastered across his career.

Something Different

2019
Apparel Engineering Foundation
